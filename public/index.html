Features

1.

Create and take Courses

We provide courses on a variety of topics to hone your skills and improve your knowlegde.You can also create your own courses to help share your skills.

2.

Rate and review Resources

You can rate and review courses and other study resources. You can also view other's reviews to help you determine the best source for you.

3.

Write and share Notes

Effective note taking helps you to remember information and aids your understanding of that information. Keeping this in mind we have a feature to write notes and save and share them.

4.

Flashcards for quick revision

Flashcards are the most effective way for motivated learners to study and retain factual knowledge. We have flashcards for each course for efficient revision.

5.

Learning paths

You can create multiple learning paths in your account. You can also share your learning path with your friends/peers by creating a Public URL for the same.

6.

Study-Buddy and friends

You can make connections by sending friend requests and the other person accepting it.
